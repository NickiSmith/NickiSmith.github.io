Bike Race Map

This map was created for my Graphic Design in Cartography course, at UW-Madison. It shows a bike race tour of the Pacific Northwest which starts and ends in Olympia, Washington, not far from the town where I grew up. Miles of Pacific coastline and scenic Cascade vistas await. Created using ArcGIS Pro, Photoshop, and Illustrator.
