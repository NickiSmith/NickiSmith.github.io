Cartography

Hi, I'm Nicki.

I make maps. I studied geography and GIS as an undergraduate, which sent me down the path to a Master's in Cartography and GIS from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My geospatial work is currently in commercial poultry, where I blend my love of geography with a lifelong interest in agriculture. In my free time, I can usually be found birdwatching, gardening, and hanging out with my dog.

Bronchitis Early Warning System

Developed for the Georgia Poultry Laboratory Network and as my practicum project for the UW-Madison Master's in Cartography and GIS, this web-based report uses interactive maps and charts to provide key information on infectious bronchitis in Georgia's commercial poultry flocks .
