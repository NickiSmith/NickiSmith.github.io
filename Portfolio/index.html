About

I grew up in the Pacific Northwest, but now call a small university town in North Georgia home. I spend most of my professional time in a laboratory where, among other things, I make maps. Presently, I’m on a mission to make better maps through my graduate studies in cartography at UW-Madison. When I’m not making maps, I like to spend my time outside — preferably with my dog/BFF — gardening, birdwatching, and hiking. Want to nerd out on maps? Feel free to send me an email.
